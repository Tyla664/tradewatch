Help & FAQs
How do I set up Telegram notifications?
Open this app through @tradewatchalert_bot on Telegram. Your account is detected automatically — no extra steps needed. Then tap Telegram Alerts in the menu and toggle it on.
Why didn't I receive an alert?
Ensure Telegram alerts are enabled and your Chat ID is correct. For setup alerts, price must first reach the setup side (breakout zone), then retest the entry level. Check the alert card status badge for current state.
What is a Trade Setup alert?
A Trade Setup alert monitors the full lifecycle of a trade — entry, stop loss, and take profit levels. It fires when price reaches your entry via a retest, then tracks TP1, TP2, TP3 and SL automatically.
How does the two-phase entry work?
For a breakout-retest setup, price must first break through the entry level (phase 1), then come back to retest it (phase 2 — entry fires). This prevents false triggers when price is already past entry when you set the alert.
Can I use altradia without the app open?
Yes. altradia monitors your alerts in the background every minute, checking all price levels and sending Telegram notifications even when the app is completely closed.
How do I log a trade?
When a setup alert reaches a final state (TP hit or SL hit), tap LOG TRADE on the alert card. For live trades, tap CLOSE then LOG TRADE. Fill in the journal form and tap SAVE TO JOURNAL.
